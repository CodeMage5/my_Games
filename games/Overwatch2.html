About Overwatch 2

Overwatch 2 is a 2022 first-person shooter game developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment.


Minimum requirements

  • OS: Windows 7/8/10 64-bit.
  • Processor: Intel Core i3 or equivalent.
  • Memory: 4GB RAM.
  • Hard Drive: 5GB space on a 7,200 RPM drive, or better.
  • Graphics: GeForce GTX 460.

How to download

  1. Download and install the Battle.net desktop app.
  2. Log into the Battle.net desktop app.
  3. Click the Overwatch 2 icon at the top. If the icon is missing, click on All Games and select the game from the list.
  4. Click Install.
