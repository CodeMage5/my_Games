About Fornite

Fortnite is a massive online multiplayer game, made by Epic Games, that released on July 25th, 2017 as Paid Early Access and as a full release game on June 30th 2020.


Minimum requirements

  • CPU: Core i3-3225 3.3 GHz
  • RAM: 4 GB
  • Intel HD 4000
  • OS: Windows 7/8/10 64-bit

How to download

  1. Download and install the Epicgames launcher.
  2. Start the Epic Games Launcher.
  3. Log in to your Epic Games account.
  4. Click Install.
